Positioning Summary

vs. Khanmigo ($4/mo): Khanmigo is cheaper but relies on Khan Academy's existing content library. We generate adaptive, personalized content per student. We have diagnostics, book review, practice mode, flashcards, and teacher cross-family visibility — none of which Khanmigo offers. Their advantage: multi-language support and image upload.

vs. IXL ($10–20/mo): IXL has the deepest drill library (17K+ skills) and solid diagnostics, but zero AI tutoring — it's a traditional adaptive platform. We match on diagnostics and surpass on everything AI-driven. Their classroom plan at $299/yr is a barrier; our teachers are free. Our $19/mo Family tier delivers more than their $20/mo 4-subject plan.

vs. Synthesis ($29/mo): Synthesis is math-only and ages 5–11. At the same $29/mo price point, our Academy tier covers all subjects, grades 6–12, uses the upgraded Sonnet model, and includes teacher mode, book review, and 8 students. We offer dramatically more value at the same price.
